PocketBuilder Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-16

PocketBuilder is a builder's tape calculator and notepad. It is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing leaves your device. PocketBuilder has no account system, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. It makes no network requests to send your data anywhere.

What is stored, and where

  • Your tapes, notes, and settings (units, fraction precision, theme, keypad layout, tabs) are saved in your browser's local storage on this device only.
  • This data never reaches us or any third party. Clearing your browser/app storage, or uninstalling the app, permanently deletes it.
  • Tapes you explicitly export or share (via the Save / Share buttons) are handed to the app or destination you choose. PocketBuilder does not retain a copy.

Offline operation

PocketBuilder installs a service worker so it runs offline. Cached files are program assets (the app itself), not your data.

Children's privacy

PocketBuilder does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change with it.
